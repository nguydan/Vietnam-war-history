<h2 id="colonial" style="color: #4b5320; border-bottom: 2px solid #4b5320; padding-bottom: 10px;">1. Colonial Foundations</h2>

The conflict was deeply rooted in the exploitation of the French colonial era. Following WWII, the return of French forces created a “double yoke” of oppression, combining colonialism with Cold War tensions.

<h2 id="mcnamara" style="color: #4b5320; border-bottom: 2px solid #4b5320; padding-bottom: 10px;">3. The McNamara Paradigm</h2>

> "If you can't measure it, you can't manage it." 

Robert S. McNamara’s tenure marked the apex of the technocratic approach. By relying on "systems analysis" and statistical data like "body counts," the human and political complexities of the war were often ignored in favor of mechanical metrics.
